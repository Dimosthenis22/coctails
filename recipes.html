Apple martini
  • 50ml Vodka
  • 20ml lime juice
  • 20ml sugar syrup
  • 30ml Apple Sourz

We put all the ingredients in a shaker with ice, we shake them for 10 seconds and we put it in a frozen martini glass

Bloody Mary
  • 60ml Vodka
  • 30ml lemon juice
  • 90ml tomato juice
  • 2 dash tabasco
  • 1 dash worst sauce
  • salt, pepper, celery

We put all the ingredients in the shaker and we stir them.Then we add the celery and some ice.

Bramble
  • 50ml Gin
  • 20ml lime juice
  • 15ml sugar syrup
  • 15 ml Creme de casis

We put all the ingredients in a shaker with ice, we shake it for ten seconds.Then we put it in a short glass with ice. On the top we add crushed ice and 5ml of creme de casis

Caipirihna
  • 60ml Cascasa
  • 25ml lime juice
  • 25ml sugar syrup

We put all the ingredients in a shaker with ice, we shake it for 10 seconds and then we put it in a short glass with ice and we add some crushed ice on top

Mai Tai
  • 40ml white Rum
  • 25ml dark-aged Rum
  • 15ml triple sec
  • 15ml Marin Orgeat
  • 15ml lime juice

We put all the ingredients in a shaker with ice,we stir it for 5 seconds and then we put it in a short glass with ice.We add some crushed ice on top.

Mojito
  • 60ml Rum
  • 30ml lime juice
  • 30ml sugar syrup
  • 5-8 mint leaves

We put all the ingredientsin a big glass. We stir them and then we add ice. We stir them once more, we put some crushed ice on top and we top up with soda.
